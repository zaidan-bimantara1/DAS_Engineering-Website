About DAS Engineering

PT. Dewata Ayu Sri is a professional MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) consulting and design company founded in 2013 by engineers Dewa and Indra. We combine expert engineering with advanced software like AutoCAD, Revit, and CAMEL to deliver efficient, cost-effective MEP solutions.

From our beginnings as a drafting service, we have grown into a full-service provider, offering MEP design, consulting, and supply tailored to client needs. Our commitment to quality and precision drives every project we handle.

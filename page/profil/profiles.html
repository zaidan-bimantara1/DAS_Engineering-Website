About Us

PT. Dewata Ayu Sri is a company specializing in the drafting, design, and consulting of Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) systems. We are supported by experienced professionals and powered by advanced engineering software such as AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Revit, CAMEL, and the E20 hourly analysis program. These tools enable us to deliver cost-effective and efficient designs, whether for 2D drafting, 3D modeling, or HVAC performance calculations.

The company began operations in February 2013 as a drafting service established by two professional engineers, Dewa and Indra. Over time, PT. Dewata Ayu Sri has expanded into a full-service provider offering MEP design, consulting, and supply based on client needs. Our commitment to quality and client satisfaction remains the foundation of every project we undertake.

Our Philosophy

PT. Dewata Ayu Sri’s philosophy is rooted in innovation, transparency, and strong teamwork. History of Company

PT. Dewata Ayu Sri was founded in 2013 by two experienced engineers, Dewa and Indra, with an initial focus on providing drafting services. From the beginning, the company set high standards for quality and technical accuracy in every project. As the industry evolved and demands increased, we gradually expanded our services into comprehensive MEP design and consulting.

Throughout our journey, we have handled a wide range of projects — from public buildings and resorts to hotels and residential villas. This growth is fueled by our dedication to responsive client service, fast project delivery, and cost-effective solutions. From its humble beginnings to its current status, PT. Dewata Ayu Sri continues to evolve as a trusted and capable MEP service provider.

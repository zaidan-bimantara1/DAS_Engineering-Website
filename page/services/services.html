SERTIFIKAT LAIK FUNGSI

The Certificate of Functional Worthiness (SLF) service from PT. Dewata Ayu Sri aims to ensure that your building has met all technical and administrative requirements set by the local government so that it is suitable for use according to its designation. The SLF acquisition process involves a series of stages including checking the building's conformity with the issued permit, testing the utility system, and verifying other supporting documents. Our team of experts has a deep understanding of applicable building regulations and licensing procedures, and establishes effective communication with relevant parties to expedite the SLF application process.

PT. Dewata Ayu Sri realizes how crucial the SLF is for the legality and operation of a building. Without an SLF, a building can be considered illegal and potentially subject to legal sanctions. With our services, you can ensure that your building meets all required safety, health, and comfort standards, providing legal certainty and peace of mind for building owners and users. We accompany you every step of the way, from document preparation, inspection, to the issuance of the SLF, ensuring an efficient and smooth process.

We believe that sustainable design is a smart and responsible long-term investment.

AUDIT UTILITIES BUILDING

Utilities Building Audit Services from PT. Dewata Ayu Sri provides an in-depth and comprehensive analysis of the performance of your building’s utility systems, including mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems. Through this audit, we evaluate energy efficiency, water usage, and overall system operational performance to identify potential inefficiencies, areas for improvement, and opportunities for long-term operational cost savings. Our audit process involves field data collection, technical analysis, and comparison to relevant performance standards.

Based on the audit results, PT. Dewata Ayu Sri will present a clear and detailed report, complete with measurable and implementable recommendations to improve efficiency and reduce the operating costs of your building. These recommendations may include repairing or replacing inefficient equipment, optimizing system operating schedules, implementing energy-efficient technologies, or changing user behavior. With this audit service, we help clients achieve significant cost savings, improve building sustainability, and create a more comfortable and efficient environment for their occupants.
